Our Homes

Affinity Children's Services provides a nurturing environment for young people at our home, Forest View, in Hednesford, Cannock. This spacious, tranquil property accommodates up to three children, offering ensuite bedrooms, lounges, a garden, and access to excellent local amenities, schools, and healthcare, ensuring comfort, safety, and personal growth.

AFFINITY

Our Homes

All of our homes are large, detached properties and based in local communities. This ensures that all children and young people in our care have access to local amenities, providing them with the same life chances as other young people in the area, who are not part of the care system.

Forest View is a tranquil, spacious home in Hednesford, Cannock, offering comfortable accommodation for up to three children. With six bedrooms, each with an ensuite, two lounges, a conservatory, and a large garden, it provides a secure, private space. Nearby amenities include schools, parks, medical services, and transport links.
