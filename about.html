About Affinity

Next Stage 4 Life provides therapeutic care to children and young people in our eight homes across the North West. We offer a holistic approach to care that covers a range of therapeutic models and incorporates a full plan for health, education and care. Our aim is to ensure that all our children, young people are given the support and opportunities to achieve successful outcomes and thrive as members of their communities.

AFFINITY

Who We Are

Next Stage 4 Life Ltd was established in 2014. It shares its central support services, working in collaboration other Next Stage companies, each providing social care solutions in their own specific areas. These other companies form the Next Stage group, click below to visit the Group Website.

Our Approach

We provide therapeutic care to children and young people between the ages of 7 and 18 in our residential children's homes. These homes are fully-registered under the Care Standards Act 2000 and we work within the legal framework set out by the Children's Homes Regulations and Quality Standards 2015 and other associated legislation. Our regulatory body is Ofsted, who perform regular inspections to ensure we are working to the appropriate standard.


Our primary model of therapy is Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT). However, we will use other therapeutic methods, depending on the individual needs of the child or young person in question. These may include psychotherapy, play therapy, or other methods. All therapy is provided by an in house therapist, employed directly by the company on a full-time basis.


Therapeutic intervention can be provided individually to the child or young person, or via intervention with the home's support staff to deliver a therapeutic environment. Depending on the circumstances, we can put together an approach that allows for both possibilities.

Other health matters can be accessed through partner organisations such as Children and Adolescent Mental Health Services (CAMHS) or universal health services.


The child or young person's education is provided through the Local Education Authority (LEA) and some of the young people in our care will be subject to an Education, Health, and Care Plan (EHCP).


All of our homes are large, detached properties that provide our residents with full access to local amenities within their community. This ensures that they can expect the same chances and aspirations as children and young people of similar age who are not in the care system.


Each child and young person will have their own individual plans tailored to meet their particular needs while in our homes. These plans are followed by our entire staff to ensure a high standard of service delivery that safeguards every child and young person in our care.

Our Mission

We care for those around us and provide crucial support to vulnerable children, young people, and adults within our communities. We inspire those around us by showing that we care – we earn their trust, we build them up, and we help them accept life's challenges. We strive to support every person in our care to achieve successful outcomes and we meaningfully engage with our communities. We support individual learning and growth within a safe and supportive working environment. We believe in the power of positive inspiration and the effect it can have on a young person's life.

Our Values

At Next Stage 4 Life, we are committed to providing the very best in both service and support to the children and young people in our care. We have a set of core values that defines how we work and shapes our approach towards our homes, our residents, and their wider relationship with the local community.

We Believe Everyone Matters

  • we see everyone as an individual, seek to understand their needs and aspirations, and value their differences
  • we encourage everyone to have a voice
  • we actively listen and respect each other's contributions

We Are Resilient

  • we view change as a challenge and an opportunity
  • we can handle stress and unpleasant feelings and engage with others for support
  • give our best efforts, no matter what and never give up

We Work As A Team

  • we work together to achieve our goals
  • we are understanding and are interested in how each other work
  • we share our time and our knowledge to innovate each other

We Inspire One Another

  • we are confident and impactful in the way we present ourselves and our company
  • we unlock each other's potential
  • we have a can-do approach to achieving our outcomes

We Strive For Excellence And Quality

  • we strive to be the best version of ourselves and inspire others to believe in themselves
  • we are always seeking opportunities for growth and reaching for the stars.
