Who We Are

Next Stage 4 Life Ltd was established in 2014. It shares its central support services, working in collaboration other Next Stage companies, each providing social care solutions in their own specific areas. These other companies form the Next Stage group, click below to visit the Group Website.

Standard Operating Procedure

We have our own standard operating procedure for making referrals for placement. These involve conducting a thorough risk assessment to determine the specific needs of the child or young person in question. We then take that assessment and consider whether we have the facilities and resources in place to provide them with the level of care that they need. These standard operating procedures ensure that all children and young people referred to us for placement are treated exactly the same as one another. Full details of our SOP are available upon request.
